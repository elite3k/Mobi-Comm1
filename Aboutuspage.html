Company History & Milestones

Founding Story

Mobi-Comm was founded in 2020 by a team of telecom enthusiasts aiming to revolutionize mobile connectivity in India. Driven by the vision of affordable, reliable communication, we started with a small office in Tech City, focusing on prepaid recharge solutions for rural and urban customers alike.

Key Achievements and Milestones

Launched India’s first AI-powered mobile recharge system in 2025, expanded to over 10 million users, and introduced 5G services in key cities, earning the “Innovative Telecom Provider of the Year” award in 2024.

Growth Journey

From a startup with 100 customers, we’ve grown to serve millions across India, adding new services like international roaming, family plans, and entertainment packages, while expanding our network to remote areas.
